( 07 ) — FAQ

Asked, answered.

01Will callers know it's an AI?

Kawlya introduces itself honestly when asked, but its voice, pacing and conversational memory feel completely natural. Most callers simply notice they got helped fast.

02Do I have to change my phone number?

No. You keep your number and simply forward calls — always, after a few rings, or only outside business hours. You can switch it off anytime.

03What happens with complicated calls?

Kawlya recognizes when a human is needed and warm-transfers the call with full context — or takes a detailed message and flags it as urgent.

04How long does setup take?

Most businesses are live within an afternoon. Connect a number, import your business info, make a test call — done.

05Is my data secure?

Calls are encrypted in transit and at rest, processed in certified data centers, and never used to train shared models. HIPAA-ready plans are available.
